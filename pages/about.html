My Story

For nearly twenty years, I've worked in the hum of CNC machines, cutting material down to a ten-thousandth of an inch. In those patterns of motion and metal, I learned discipline, intention, and the beauty of exactness.

With nearly two decades of machining experience across Billet Industries, TAIT Towers, and Jones Manufacturing, I have developed a deep respect for rigor, accuracy, and the integrity of well-crafted systems. From aerospace components requiring ±0.0001" tolerances to entertainment rigging for global tours, I discovered that precision is a form of prayer.

Later, through recovery, psychedelic healing, and ordination, I entered another apprenticeship — one of the heart. There I learned presence, surrender, gratitude, and the interconnectedness that mycelial wisdom reveals so clearly. My ministerial work and psychedelic-informed spiritual path have shaped a parallel commitment to healing, self-awareness, and compassionate leadership.

Today, I stand at the confluence of those paths, building sacred technology: zero-knowledge governance, ritual intelligence, soulbound identity, and spiritual-technical ecosystems like Ethereal Offering.

As co-creator of Ethereal Offering, I am developing mycelial-inspired infrastructures of gratitude, ritual remembrance, and soulbound identity. This isn't just another blockchain project — it's a living altar where code becomes prayer and mycelium becomes architecture.

My work reflects a simple belief: that craft, code, and consciousness are all expressions of the same underlying intelligence — and that when technology is built with intention and reverence, it becomes a tool for collective upliftment.
