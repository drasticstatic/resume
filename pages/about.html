My Story

My journey weaves through three worlds: the precision of engineering, the transformation of recovery and psychedelic healing, and the emerging frontier of sacred technology.

For nearly twenty years, I shaped matter with microns of precision — turning metal into form, structure, and purpose across Billet Industries, TAIT Towers, and Jones Manufacturing. In that work, I learned discipline, intention, and the quiet language of machines.

Through ordination, ceremony, and contemplative practice, I stepped into another apprenticeship — one of the heart. There, I learned presence, gratitude, humility, and the interconnectedness underlying all life.

Today, I bring these paths together by building technologies of healing and remembrance: zero-knowledge governance, spiritual identity, ritual intelligence, and gratitude-based ecosystems like Ethereal Offering.
