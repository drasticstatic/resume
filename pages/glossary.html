A Aleo

A Layer 1 blockchain focused on zero-knowledge cryptography and privacy. Uses Leo programming language for writing ZK circuits. Ethereal Offering uses Aleo for anonymous governance and private voting.

A AMM (Automated Market Maker)

A decentralized exchange protocol that uses liquidity pools instead of order books. Users can swap tokens directly from pools, and liquidity providers earn fees.

B Blockchain

A distributed ledger that records transactions across many computers. Immutable, transparent, and decentralized.

B Blockchain Interoperability

Ability of different blockchain networks to communicate and share data with each other, enabling cross-chain transactions and applications.

B Blockchain Oracle

Service that provides external data to smart contracts. Chainlink oracles provide price feeds for collateral systems.

C Ceremonial Offering

The act of burning ETHO tokens at the Sacred Altar as a spiritual practice, inspired by ancient burnt offering traditions.

C CNC Lathe

Computer-controlled machine tool that rotates workpiece for precision turning operations. Capable of complex geometries with tight tolerances.

C Consensus Mechanism

Protocol used by blockchain networks to agree on the validity of transactions. Common types include Proof of Work and Proof of Stake.

C Cryptocurrency

Digital or virtual currency secured by cryptography, making it nearly impossible to counterfeit. Operates independently of central banks.

C Crypto Wallet

Digital tool for storing, sending, and receiving cryptocurrencies. Contains public and private keys for blockchain transactions.

D DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization)

Organization represented by rules encoded as smart contracts, controlled by members rather than central authority. Enables transparent, democratic governance on the blockchain.

D dApp (Decentralized Application)

Application that runs on a decentralized network, typically blockchain, rather than centralized servers. Offers censorship resistance and user control.

D DeFi (Decentralized Finance)

Financial services built on blockchain without traditional intermediaries like banks. Includes lending, borrowing, trading, and yield farming protocols.

D DM (Daily Mushroom)

The daily rewards token earned through check-ins, ceremonies, and participation. Used for gas fees, breeding, and unlocking features.

E ERC-20

The standard for fungible tokens on Ethereum. PSILO, MDAO, ETHO, PSD, and DM are all ERC-20 tokens.

E ERC-721

The standard for non-fungible tokens (NFTs) on Ethereum. POB and SHROOM are ERC-721 tokens.

E ETHO (Ethereal Offering Token)

The ceremonial burn token used at the Sacred Altar. Deflationary supply, earned through participation.

E Ethereum

A Layer 1 blockchain with smart contract functionality. Primary blockchain for Ethereal Offering's token ecosystem.

E Eternal Flame

A 365+ day streak of daily offerings or check-ins. Earns 2x-3x rewards.

G Gas Fees

The transaction fee paid to blockchain validators. On Ethereum, gas fees vary based on network congestion ($1-$100).

G Governance Token

Cryptocurrency that gives holders voting rights in decentralized protocols, allowing community-driven decision making and protocol upgrades.

H Harvest

Burning a Mushroom NFT to receive DM rewards. Mature mushrooms can be harvested for 50-10,000 DM depending on rarity.

H Holacracy

A decentralized governance structure with autonomous circles. Ethereal Offering DAO has 4 circles: Core, Development, Community, and Spiritual.

L Layer 1 (L1)

A base blockchain like Ethereum, TON, or Aleo. Processes transactions and maintains consensus.

L Layer 2 (L2)

A scaling solution built on top of Layer 1. Processes transactions faster and cheaper, then settles to L1.

L Liquidity Pool

A smart contract holding two tokens that users can trade against. Liquidity providers deposit tokens and earn fees.

L LLM (Large Language Model)

AI model trained on vast amounts of text data to understand and generate human-like text. Powers applications like ChatGPT and our Oracle of Fruit.

M Mastercam

Industry-leading CAM (Computer-Aided Manufacturing) software for creating CNC toolpaths and programs. Used for mill, lathe, and multi-axis machining.

M Mazatrol

Conversational CNC programming language used on Mazak machine tools. Allows intuitive part programming through graphical interface and conversational prompts.

M MDAO (Mushroom DAO Token)

The governance token that grants voting rights in the DAO. Earned by staking PSILO or making offerings.

M MPC (Multi-Party Computation)

Cryptographic protocol allowing multiple parties to jointly compute a function while keeping inputs private. Used for secure key management and collaborative computation.

M Mycelial Networks

Decentralized organizational structures inspired by fungal networks. Emphasizes interconnection, distributed intelligence, and mutual support without hierarchy.

N NFT (Non-Fungible Token)

A unique digital asset on the blockchain. Each NFT is distinct and not interchangeable. POB and SHROOM are NFTs.

P POB (Proof of Burn)

A soulbound NFT minted when you make an offering at the Sacred Altar. Permanent receipt of your burn.

P Precision Metrology

Science of measurement ensuring parts meet specified tolerances, often to ¬±0.0001" accuracy. Uses CMMs, micrometers, and optical comparators.

P Proof of You

The concept that your participation is proof of your existence and value. Not proof of work or stake, but proof of presence.

P PSD (Psanctuary Dollar)

The stablecoin of the ecosystem, pegged to $1 USD. Used for stable trading pairs and treasury reserves.

P PSILO (Psilocybin Token)

The crowdsale fundraising token. Fixed supply of 10M. Stake to earn MDAO.

Q Quorum

The minimum participation required for a vote to be valid. Standard proposals need 10% quorum, Critical need 40%.

R Recovery

The process of healing from addiction or trauma. Ethereal Offering integrates 12-step principles and recovery support.

R Ritual Intelligence

AI systems designed to support spiritual practices, ceremonies, and contemplative traditions. Combines technology with sacred wisdom for community healing.

S Sacred Altar

The smart contract where ETHO tokens are burned ceremonially. Mints POB NFTs and distributes MDAO rewards.

S Smart Contracts

Self-executing contracts with terms directly written into code. Automatically execute when predetermined conditions are met, eliminating need for intermediaries.

S Solidity

Object-oriented programming language for writing smart contracts on Ethereum and EVM-compatible blockchains. Syntax similar to JavaScript and C++.

S Soulbound Tokens

Non-transferable NFTs that represent identity, credentials, or achievements tied to a specific wallet address. Cannot be sold or transferred.

S Spiritual Hashrate

A formula representing spiritual effort: (Presence + Service + Recovery¬≤) / Ego

S Staking

Locking tokens in a smart contract to earn rewards. Stake PSILO to earn MDAO.

T Tokenomics

Economic model and incentive structure of a cryptocurrency token, including supply, distribution, utility, and governance mechanisms.

T TON (The Open Network)

A Layer 1 blockchain integrated with Telegram. Ethereal Offering uses TON for the Telegram mini-app and daily mining.

T 12-Step

A recovery program based on spiritual principles. Ethereal Offering integrates 12-step concepts into blockchain mechanics.

W Web3

The decentralized internet built on blockchain. Emphasizes user ownership and control.

Y Yield Farming

DeFi strategy of lending or staking cryptocurrency to earn rewards, often in the form of additional tokens or interest payments.

Z Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZK)

Cryptographic method allowing one party to prove to another that a statement is true without revealing any information beyond the validity of the statement itself. Essential for privacy-preserving blockchain applications.
